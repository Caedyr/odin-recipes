Spam Risotto

Even MORE spam!

Okay, I know how this looks, but listen. Spam's really good! Risotto is also really good! And with a little bit of math we can figure, putting one really good thing with another really good thing is gonna make it really, REALLY good.

Trust me. I've used a calculator.

Ingredient List

  • 4 tablespoons butter, divided
  • One can of spam, diced small
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 6 cups of chicken stock, warmed
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of freshly chopped herbs, such as parsley or thyme

Recipe Steps

  1. Put a large saucepan over medium heat, melting 1 tablespoon of butter.
  2. Add spam. Cook for 3 minutes until lightly browned.
  3. Remove the spam, and reserve. Add the rest of the butter to the pan to melt.
  4. Stir in the onion, cooking for 4 minutes until the onion is transparent.
  5. Stir the rice into the mixture, cooking 2 minutes until the rice begins to toast.
  6. Transfer two ladles of the warmed chicken stock into the pan, stirring the rice and stock together.
  7. Cook until the rice begins to soak up the stock, maintaining an even heat.
  8. Continue adding the stock one ladle at a time until the mixture is creamy.
  9. Remove from heat. Stir in cheese, herbs, and reserved spam.
