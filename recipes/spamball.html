Fried Spam Balls

I'm not being paid to advertise!

Really, I'm not! I just like spam a lot. And why not put together an entire list of ways to make it? It's thematic and delicious and I will not hear otherwise.

Ingredients List

  • 1 cup of diced spam
  • 2 cups cooked sushi rice
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/2 cup shredded nori
  • 1/2 cup nori powder
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe Steps

  1. Heat frying oil to 360 Â°F
  2. In a large bowl, add all of the ingredients except the breadcrumbs and mix them well
  3. Using your hands, roll mixture into 1 1/2" rice balls
  4. On a large plate, spread panko crumbs and 2 tablespoons chopped parsley and mix well
  5. Roll rice balls in panko crumbs
  6. Deep fry rice balls until golden brown
  7. Place on paper towel lined plate to cool
  8. Place the cooled rice ball on a plate and garnish with spicy ranch and nori powder
